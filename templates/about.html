Web Design Basics project

Macedonian Music Discovery

A project idea shaped by my second-semester experience at FINKI and the opportunity to build a practical web app instead of the second midterm exam.

My context

I am a student at FINKI, in the second semester of the first year of my Bachelor's degree in Application of Information Technologies (Примена на информациски технологии). After scoring highly on the first midterm, I qualified for the project-based option for the second midterm.

Project idea

The assigned topic was Macedonian Music Discovery: a browser for Macedonian artists with filters by genre, decade, and region, plus AI-generated hover descriptions to help users explore music more easily.

Technology direction

The recommended stack for the project is HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python. That combination gives the front end enough flexibility for dynamic filtering and interaction, while Python can support the smarter background logic.

My point of view

I wanted to create a web application that makes discovering local performers clear and enjoyable through filtering by music genre, decade, and geographic region. The main focus is easy discovery of new Macedonian music, supported by Python and AI in the background for dynamically generating and displaying artist descriptions. The interface is planned as a modern, responsive card-based design, where content loads dynamically without page refreshes. Beyond AI descriptions, each artist profile will also include a basic discography, links to official streaming platforms such as Spotify and YouTube, and recommendations for similar local artists to round out the user experience. I also plan to add personalization so the application can suggest content based on preferred genres, saved favorite artists, and individual interests.

Future plans

The project can grow into a more personalized music discovery tool with saved favorites, tailored suggestions, and richer artist profiles as the dataset expands.
